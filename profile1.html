The five personality traits:

The scores are rated in percent wise up to 100 %

 

1. openness 42%

2. conscientiousness 33%

3. extraversion 15%

4. agreeableness 65%

5. neuroticism 100%

 

openness

 

people who have a higher percentage under this are comfortable with abstract ideas,  people who are lower in openness are more strategic, concrete thinker who prefer exploring real world fact driven ideas

 

conscientiousness

 

People who are higher in conscientiousness are hardworking, responsible, able to stay focused.  People who are lower on conscientiousness lack ability to concentrate, less hardworking, disorganised.

 

Extraversion

 

people who are high in extraversion are: friendly, enthusiastic, excitable

people who are low in extraversion are: reserved, introspective, calm, quiet

 

agreeableness

 

people who are higher in agreeableness are: cooperative, helpful, sympathetic

people who are higher in agreeableness are:competitive, argumentative, brash

 

neuroticism

 

people who are higher in neuroticism struggle with negative emotions, have high anxiety, self conscious

people who are lower in neuroticism optimistic, self confident, carefree.
